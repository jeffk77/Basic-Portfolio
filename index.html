About Me



I've been a "computer guy" since I was 4 years old, sitting at an Apple IIc computer. My Dad taught me early programming and design and gave me the chance to experiment early on.


Having that kind of young training and experience gave me the freedom to learn at any pace I could, and I loved to consume information. At that age, it consisted mostly of home tech support, but lessons are lessons!


As my career has progressed, so has my tech work. With each successive job, I brought a new skillset to bear. I began with early desktop publishing, and later added simple web designs. Eventually things grew to expansive databases and eCommerce platforms. As a life-long tech "nerd", I'm excited to see what my career will progress to next as a Full Stack Developer.
